---
title: "Social"
description: "Trading is better with friends"
---

Social connects everything on the platform: who you follow, who you watch, who you compete against, and who you trade alongside.

Every trade, match, and rank-up is social by default. Your activity lives on the feed, your reputation is public, and your community grows as you perform.

- **Feed** -- see what traders around you are doing in real-time
- **Clans** -- team up and compete together
- **Following** -- track the traders you care about
- **Chat** -- talk to opponents, clanmates, and friends directly on the platform
