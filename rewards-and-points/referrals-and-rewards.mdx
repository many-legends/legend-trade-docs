Every Legend user gets a unique referral link (reflink). Share it anywhere — when someone signs up through your link, you earn a percentage of their trading fees for as long as they use Legend.

## How It Works

1. **Share your reflink** — find it in your profile
2. **They sign up and trade** — your referral is tracked automatically
3. **You earn** — a cut of their fees flows to you continuously

## Clan Tiers

Clan Leaders get 5% of fees from all members in their clan.

## Base Rate

By default, you earn **10%** of your referred users' fees.

## Bonus Tiers

Deposit more to Legend to unlock higher referral rates:

| Deposit Amount | Referral Rate |
| --- | --- |
| Base | 10% |
| \$5,000\+ | 12.5% |
| \$50,000\+ | 15% |
| \$250,000\+ | 17.5% |
| \$500,000\+ | 20% |

Your tier is based on your net deposits (deposits minus withdrawals) to Legend. Higher net deposits unlock better rates across all your referrals. Withdrawals lower your net deposit amount, which may reduce your tier.

All of these bonuses stack: meaning, if you are eligible for **20% referral rate**, and are a Clan Leader (**+5%**), you may receive **up to 25% of fees** for people that are both your referral and a Clan Member.

**External Chart Notes**

Sharing chart notes and feed items externally also counts as referrals. Anyone who signs up through a shared note or feed link is automatically tracked as your referral.

## Clan Rewards

As a clan leader, you earn a share of your clan's trading fees. This is separate from referral rewards -- you earn from all member trades while they're in your clan.

- Clan leaders earn **5%** of builder fee revenue generated by their members' trades
- Only trades made while a user is a member of your clan count
- Your own trades as leader are excluded
- Clan rewards can be claimed from your profile

<Note>
  To create a clan, you need over $100,000 in lifetime notional trading volume on Legend. See [Clans](/clans) for more details.
</Note>
