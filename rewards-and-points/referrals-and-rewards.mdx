Every Legend user gets a unique referral link (reflink). Share it anywhere — when someone signs up through your link, you earn a percentage of their trading fees for as long as they use Legend.

## How It Works

1. **Share your reflink** — find it in your profile
2. **They sign up and trade** — your referral is tracked automatically
3. **You earn** — a cut of their fees flows to you continuously

## Base Rate

By default, you earn **10%** of your referred users' fees.

## Bonus Tiers

Deposit more to Legend to unlock higher referral rates:

| Deposit Amount | Referral Rate |
|----------------|---------------|
| Base           | 10%           |
| $5,000+        | 12.5%         |
| $50,000+       | 15%           |
| $250,000+      | 17.5%        |
| $500,000+      | 20%           |

Your tier is based on your total deposits to Legend. Higher deposits unlock better rates across all your referrals.
