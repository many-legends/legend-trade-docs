Clans are Legend's team system -- compete together, climb together.

**Clan Leaders get 5% of fees from all members in their clan.**

- To create a clan, you must exceed $100,000 in lifetime notional trading volume on Legend
- Capped at 50 members -- you can only be in one clan at a time
- Every clan has its own ELO, tracked separately from individual rank
- Launch clan wars against rivals and climb the seasonal clan leaderboard
- Each clan has its own chat, member list, and shared stats
- Invite friends or recruit from the leaderboard

Your clan is your identity on Legend -- who you ride with.
