Legend applies a uniform 0.05% (5 bps) platform fee on perpetual futures trades, on top of Hyperliquid's volume-tiered network fees.

What to know about Legend fees:

- Fees are based on your account's rolling 14-day trading volume.
- Fees are assessed at the end of each day (UTC).
- Maker rebates are paid out continuously on each trade directly to your wallet.
- Staking HYPE on Hyperliquid reduces your network fees.
- A portion of the Legend platform fee funds [referral payouts and clan leader rewards](/rewards-and-points/referrals-and-rewards).

## Fee Schedule

The table below shows total fees for Legend traders (Hyperliquid network fee + 0.05% Legend platform fee).

| Tier | 14d Volume | Taker | Maker |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | \< $5M | 0.095% | 0.065% |
| 1 | $5M+ | 0.090% | 0.063% |
| 2 | $25M+ | 0.088% | 0.062% |
| 3 | $100M+ | 0.085% | 0.060% |
| 4 | $500M+ | 0.083% | 0.058% |
| 5 | $1B+ | 0.080% | 0.056% |
| 6 | $3B+ | 0.078% | 0.055% |
| 7 | $5B+ | 0.075% | 0.053% |

Sub-account volume counts toward your master account, and all sub-accounts share the same fee tier.

## Fee Breakdown

Each trade fee has two components:

| Component | Fee |
| --- | --- |
| Legend platform fee | 0.05% (fixed) |
| Hyperliquid network fee | Variable (volume-tiered) |

The Hyperliquid network fee decreases as your 14-day trading volume increases. Staking HYPE on Hyperliquid provides additional discounts on the network fee. See [Hyperliquid's fee documentation](https://hyperliquid.gitbook.io/hyperliquid-docs/trading/fees) for the full staking discount schedule.

## Referred User Cashback

Users who signed up through a referral link earn **10% cashback** on Legend platform fees from losing trades. This cashback accrues automatically and can be claimed from the Rewards section in your profile.
