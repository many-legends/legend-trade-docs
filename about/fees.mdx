Legend trades are executed on [Hyperliquid](https://hyperliquid.xyz). Your total fee on each trade has two parts: the **Hyperliquid network fee** and the **Legend platform fee**.

## Hyperliquid Network Fees

Hyperliquid charges volume-tiered maker/taker fees based on your rolling 14-day trading volume, assessed at the end of each day in UTC.

There are separate fee schedules for perpetuals and spot. Both count toward a single volume tier, with spot volume weighted double:

**14d weighted volume** = (14d perps volume) + 2 &times; (14d spot volume)

Stable-pair spot markets (e.g. USDC/USDT) are charged at **0.2&times;** the standard spot rate.

**Maker rebates** are paid out on each fill directly to your trading wallet when your maker rate is negative.

Sub-account volume counts toward your master account, and all sub-accounts share the same fee tier.

### Staking Discount

Staking HYPE on Hyperliquid reduces your network trading fees. See [Hyperliquid's fee documentation](https://hyperliquid.gitbook.io/hyperliquid-docs/trading/fees) for the full staking discount schedule.

## Legend Platform Fee

A platform fee (builder code fee) is charged on every trade placed through Legend, on top of the Hyperliquid network fee:

| Market | Platform Fee |
| --- | --- |
| Perpetuals | 0.05% |
| Spot | 0.25% (sells only) |

<Note>
  The spot platform fee applies to **sells only**. On Hyperliquid, builder fees are collected in the quote asset (USDC), so they apply to the sell side of a spot trade (where you receive USDC) and not to the buy side.
</Note>

A portion of this fee funds [referral payouts and clan leader rewards](/rewards-and-points/referrals-and-rewards).

## Total Fee

Your total fee per trade is the sum of both components:

**Total fee** = Hyperliquid network fee + Legend platform fee

For example, a taker perpetual trade at the base volume tier costs:

- Hyperliquid taker fee: 0.045%
- Legend platform fee: 0.05%
- **Total: 0.095%**

## Referred User Cashback

Users who signed up through a referral link earn **10% cashback** on Legend platform fees from losing trades. This cashback accrues automatically and can be claimed from the Rewards section in your profile.
