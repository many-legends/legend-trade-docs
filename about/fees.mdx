Legend is built on [Hyperliquid](https://hyperliquid.xyz), one of the highest-performance onchain order books available. All trades are executed directly on the Hyperliquid L1 — your assets remain in your own wallet and are never custodied by Legend.

Legend applies a small platform fee on top of Hyperliquid's volume-tiered network fees. The more you trade, the lower your total fees become.

What to know about Legend fees:

- **Volume-tiered pricing** — Fees are based on your rolling 14-day trading volume, assessed at the end of each day (UTC). Higher volume unlocks lower fee tiers automatically.
- **Maker rebates** — When you add liquidity (maker orders), rebates are paid out continuously on each trade directly to your wallet.
- **Sub-account sharing** — Sub-account volume counts toward your master account, and all sub-accounts share the same fee tier.
- **HYPE staking discounts** — Staking HYPE on Hyperliquid further reduces your network fees. See [Hyperliquid's fee documentation](https://hyperliquid.gitbook.io/hyperliquid-docs/trading/fees) for the full staking discount schedule.

## Fee Breakdown

Each trade fee has two components:

| Component | Fee |
| --- | --- |
| Legend platform fee | Fixed per trade |
| Hyperliquid network fee | Variable (volume-tiered) |

The Hyperliquid network fee decreases as your 14-day trading volume increases. Combined with HYPE staking discounts, active traders can significantly reduce their total trading costs.

## Referral Fee Discounts

If you signed up through a referral link, you're eligible for discounts on your trading fees. A portion of the platform fees you pay are returned to you as claimable rebates. These rebates accrue automatically on your trades and can be claimed directly in the app from the Rewards section in your profile.

For more details on how referrals work and additional ways to earn, see [Referrals and Rewards](/rewards-and-points/referrals-and-rewards).
